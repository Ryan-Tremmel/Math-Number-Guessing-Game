Thank you for playing my game! Here's how to play:

A random number between 1 and 200 will be generated, and it is your goal to determine what that number is using one of the four mathematical operations. You will have 12 lives, and can only use each operation 3 times. Don't worry about which number to put where for substraction or division, as it will take the larger number and operate on the smaller regardless of which input you place it in. If your guess is close by 25 or 10 in either direction, the game will inform you. Goodluck!
